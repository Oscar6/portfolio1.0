Burnify is a tool that assists users in burning calories via Spotify playlists curated by our team.
With a researched formula, the tool calculates a user's maximum BPM needed to reach their FBZ (fat burning zone), a song's BPM, and provides the estimated calorie count that they are expected to burn per song and playlist.
